About Haryana

Haryana has been a cradle of Indian culture and civilization. Indian traditions regard this region as the matrix of creation of northern altar' where Brahma performed the pristine sacrifice and created the universe. The rich Haryanvi culture is characterised by the hookahs and the charpoys, the vivid fairs and the swaying paddy fields; Haryana is one of the wealthiest states in India and is one of the most economically developed regions in South Asia.

Some of the most popular tourist destinations in Haryana include:

  • The Millennium City Centre Gurugram (formerly known as HUDA City Centre) is a terminal station on the Yellow Line of the Delhi Metro. It is an elevated station and is located in Gurgaon in the National Capital Region of India. Gurgaon is strategically located near the national capital region of Delhi and has become a hub for industrial, financial and lifestyle advancements.


  • Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is an ideal weekend getaway from Delhi for every bird and nature lover. This place houses multiple species of migratory birds, almost 250 species, and you can spot them, especially during winters. You can watch distinct species of birds coming from parts of Siberia, Europe, and Central Asia from the towers built at different points.


  • Pinjore Garden is located 600 metres above sea level, Pinjore Garden is considered one of the best gardens in India. This garden is adorned with fountains and vibrant flowers. You can also travel to nearby attractions such as Mughal Garden and Bhima Devi.


  • Morni Hills is the only hill station and one of the prefered places to visit in Haryana. It is located on the outskirts of Panchkula, Haryana, near Chandigarh. It is popular among tourists and locals as a great picnic spot.


  • Karnal Hills is situated on the banks of the Yamuna River, Karnal is touted as the 'Rice Bowl of India'. Although it has limited tourist spots, you can still find some notable spots, such as Karna Lake and Kalander Shah's Tomb. Besides, it houses top-notch international standard institutions, including the Directorate of Wheat Research and National Dairy Research Institute.

    Kurukshetra

    Kurushetra, also known as Dharmakshetra, is popularly called the 'Land of the Bhagavad Gita'. It is located 170 kilometres north of Delhi and is known for the battle between Pandavas and Kauravas of Mahabharat. The land where the epic battle of Mahabhrata took place, is an important pilgrim site and a holy land. The place is also where the Krishna preached Bhagawad Gita to Arjuna and sage Manu wrote Manusmriti. This place is a must-visit for people who are interested in history and Indian mythology.

  • Best Time to Visit:September to MArch
  • Tourist Attractions:Brahma Sarovar, Jyotisar, Krishna Museum

    • Mahendargah

    Mahendergarh district is situated at the extreme end of the south-west end of Haryana state. Its west-south borders and a large part of the eastern boundary, the Rajasthan region and the rest of the eastern boundary, along with the districts of Haryana, Rewari and northern part of Bhiwani district. District Mahendragarh is famous for historical and religious heritage. Narnaul has 14 major historical sites in the city, of which three monuments are preserved by the Central Archeology Department and 11 Haryana State Archeology Department.

  • Best Time to Visit:October to March
  • Popular things to do:Dhosi Hill, Tripolia Gateway, Jal Mahal

    • Murthal

    Murthal is a village located in the Sonipat district of the Indian state of Haryana, near the Delhi-NCR region. It is famous for its dhabas, which are roadside restaurants serving delicious Punjabi cuisine. This village in the Sonipat district is a paradise for all food lovers as it is filled with 50 and above restaurants and dhabas. People from Delhi-NCR and beyond often visit Murthal for its famous dhabas and enjoy the traditional North Indian food, especially the parathas, which are a popular choice among visitors.

  • Best Time to Visit:During Winters
  • Popular things to do:Mojoland, Jurassic Park Inn
